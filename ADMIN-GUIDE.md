# Nasc Consulting — Website Admin Guide

## How to Edit Your Website

Your website has a simple admin panel that lets you update all the text, images, and content without touching any code.

---

## Step 1: Go to the Admin Page

Open your browser and go to:

**https://nascconsulting.netlify.app/admin/**

(Make sure to include the `/admin/` at the end)

---

## Step 2: Log In

1. Click **"Log in"** (not Sign up)
2. Enter the **email** and **password** you set up
3. Click **"Log in"**

---

## Step 3: Edit Your Content

After logging in, you'll see a screen called **"Site Content"**.

1. Click on **"Edit Website Content"**
2. You'll see sections that match your website — each one can be expanded:

| Section | What You Can Edit |
|---------|-------------------|
| **SEO & Meta** | Page title and description (what shows up in Google search results) |
| **Hero Section** | The big tagline, subtitle, and button text at the top of the site |
| **About Section** | Your bio paragraphs, headshot photo, and personal quote |
| **Services Section** | Your list of consulting services and their descriptions |
| **Client Focus Areas** | The industries/areas you serve |
| **Community & Civic Leadership** | Your civic area cards — each with a title and description |
| **Why Nasc Consulting** | The "Why Work With Me" section — four cards highlighting what sets you apart |
| **Contact Section** | Phone number, email, location, and contact form messages |
| **Footer** | The tagline and info at the bottom of the page |

3. Click on any section to expand it and see the fields you can edit

---

## Step 4: Make Your Changes

- Click into any text field and type your changes
- To edit your **headshot photo**: click the image field, then upload a new photo
- To **add a new service or list item**: click the "Add" button at the bottom of that list
- To **remove an item**: click the "X" or delete button next to it
- To **reorder items**: drag and drop them into the order you want

---

## Step 5: Save and Publish

When you're done making changes:

1. Click the **"Publish"** button (top-right area of the screen)
2. Select **"Publish now"**
3. Wait about 30–60 seconds for the website to rebuild
4. Refresh your website to see the changes live

---

## Tips

- **You can't break anything** — the admin panel only lets you edit content, not the design or layout
- **Changes take about 30–60 seconds** to appear on the live site after publishing
- **You can edit as often as you like** — there's no limit
- **If you get logged out**, just go back to the admin URL and log in again
- **Bookmark the admin page** so you can find it easily: `https://nascconsulting.netlify.app/admin/`
