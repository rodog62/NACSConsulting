ABOUT

Robert Crain

Over the course of my career I've worn a lot of hats — leader, strategist, communicator, advocate. What ties it all together is a genuine curiosity about people and a draw toward work that doesn't fit neatly into one box.

I've helped organizations work through hard problems, build stronger teams, and find clarity when the path forward isn't obvious. Where I've consistently added the most value is at the intersection of organizations and the people they serve.

I'm at my best when I'm working alongside people who are trying to figure something out — bringing whatever is most useful to the table, whether that's experience, a fresh perspective, or just a steady presence in the middle of a complicated moment.

"Nasc" is the Irish word for "link" or "connection" — a nod to Irish heritage and a reflection of work that has always been about people and relationships.
